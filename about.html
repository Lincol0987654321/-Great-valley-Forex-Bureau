About Greatvalley Forex

Trusted financial partner for currency exchange and international money transfers in Malawi since 2015.

Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2015, Greatvalley Forex Bureau Limited has grown to become one of Blantyre's most trusted providers of foreign exchange services. Our mission is simple: to provide secure, competitive, and reliable currency exchange solutions that empower individuals, businesses, and organizations across Malawi.

As a licensed forex bureau regulated by the Reserve Bank of Malawi, we operate with the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and customer service. Our team of experienced financial professionals is dedicated to helping our clients navigate the complexities of international finance with confidence.

Our Journey

2015

Establishment

Greatvalley Forex Bureau Limited was founded in Blantyre, Malawi, with a vision to provide accessible and trustworthy foreign exchange services to the local community.

2018

Licensing & Expansion

Obtained full licensing from the Reserve Bank of Malawi and expanded operations to serve corporate clients and international remittance customers.

2022

Digital Transformation

Launched digital platforms and enhanced online services, becoming one of the first forex bureaus in Malawi to offer real-time rate tracking and online support.

2026

National Leadership

Recognized as a leader in financial inclusion and innovation, serving over 50,000 customers annually across Malawi with industry-leading exchange rates and service quality.
